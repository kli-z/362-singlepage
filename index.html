Security vulnerabilities are shockingly commonplace. With x new vulnerabilities in software per year, it is becoming increasingly important that users of all types be educated on when exploits are found so that account credentials can be changed (or whatever other sort of action needs to be taken).

This site aims to assist users such as yourself in taking the proper actions to keep you informed on recent security vulnerabilities. All we ask is an email address. With it, default behavior in handling it will be to send you emails when popular enough sites or services are attacked; however, we also allow you to receive daily emails in a newsletter fashion, or to list sites for which you would like to be notified. For more information on how your email address will be handled, please visit our privacy policy statement. Alternatively, you may proceed directly to signup.
